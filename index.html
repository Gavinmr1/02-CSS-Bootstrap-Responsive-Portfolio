About Gavin Riggs

Hello name is Gavin Riggs. I am a twenty four year old student at the University of Kansas. I am studying front and back end web development with the intention of finding a career path that I can enjoy and will allow me to meet my full potential. I have had many jobs in different fields of work so I have gained a lot of experience with people, and problem solving, as well as working with my hands. Anyone who knows me knows I have a green thumb. I collect cacti, succulents, and an assortment of other plants. I enjoy playing the cello and ukulele. I love the outdoors so some of my favorite activities include camping, canoeing, hiking, hunting, and fishing.
